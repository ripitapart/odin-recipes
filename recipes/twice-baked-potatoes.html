Twice Baked Potatoes

Follow the recipe perfectly if you want yours to look like this

Quick easy recipe for great baked potatoes. Don't mess it up

Ingredients
  • potatoe
  • sour cream
  • bacon
  • Milk
  • green onion
  • cheese
Steps
  1. bake the potatoe at 350 for 1hour
  2. take it out, scoop the insides and smash until its a red mush
  3. keep the heart of the potatoe for later
  4. mix mashed organs with sour cream, bacon, milk, green onion, and cheese
  5. sprinke some cheese on top, then put the heart on top
  6. bake again for 30 minutes
  7. enjoy!
