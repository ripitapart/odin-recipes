Candied oranges

Who doesn't love come candied orange peel?

All you need is 3 ingredients. Easy!

Ingredients
  • 2 Oranges
  • sugar
  • water
Steps
  1. skin the oranges. They will scream but ignore them
  2. boil some water
  3. toss the orange peels in. The screams shoudl stop
  4. boil for 5 minutes, then take out the peels and cut off the remaining pith
  5. You can see inside the orange now, but dont get overwhelmed
  6. boil sugar water. Equal parts sugar and water
  7. toss the peels in and boil until the syrup gets thick
  8. coat finished peels in sugar
  9. enjoy!
